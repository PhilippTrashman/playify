KEY FEATURES

Full Spotify Integration

Play any Spotify playlist, including official curated ones like "This Is" and "Radar" that most bots can't handle.

Real-Time Audio Filters

Transform your music with the /filter command to apply effects like slowed, reverb, nightcore, and more.

Intelligent Autoplay

Never let the music stop. Playify automatically adds similar songs to the queue when your current one ends.

No Limits, Truly Free

No paywalled features, no playlist limits. Playify is 100% free and open source for everyone, forever.

Advanced Controls

Manage your queue like a pro with
/playnext, /shuffle, and /loop commands for a tailored experience.

Karaoke & Lyrics

Enjoy karaoke with synced lyrics and sing along to your favorite songs.
